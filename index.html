women++

I am currently a part of the team at the women++ in computing society at the City University Of London. Through launching the Women++ initiative, it should hope to establish the Women in Computing agenda be seen as a necessary and integral part of the department's strategy and activities. In addition, to improve the gender balance in computing our Department will thrive as a department we should be aiming to change cultural and environmental factors which present barriers to attitudes that challenge female participation in Computing. , including with wider stakeholders within the university’s infrastructure.
We hope to achieve this mission through activities such as networking opportunities, external partnerships, Public discourse and tackling misconceptions, mentorship schemes, workshops to shape micro-cultures, socially- mediated learning opportunities, etc.
We were able to launch our society successfully on 24/05/2023
